Silent Vent Companion

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Silent Vent Companion (SVC)?

VC is a platform designed to provide students, particularly PSHS-SRC scholars, with a secure space to express their emotions openly and without judgment.

Is my information kept confidential on SVC?

Absolutely. Your privacy is a top priority. All information shared on SVC is kept confidential and secure.

Are there any restrictions on what I can share on SVC?

SVC encourages open expression but insists on a respectful and supportive atmosphere. Any harmful or offensive content is not permitted.

How can I report inappropriate content or behavior on SVC?

SVC has reporting features in place. If you encounter any inappropriate content or behavior, you can report it, and the team will take appropriate action.

Can I remain anonymous on SVC?

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous while sharing your thoughts and feelings on SVC.

Is SVC available to everyone?

SVC is primarily aimed at PSHS-SRC scholars but welcomes anyone seeking a supportive space to express their emotions respectfully and empathetically.
