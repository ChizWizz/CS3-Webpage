Silent Vent Companion

About Us

Mission Statement

Silent Vent Companion (SVC) is dedicated to providing a secure platform for students to express and connect over emotions freely and without judgment. Our mission is to create a safe haven for students to vent, share, and find support within a trusted community.

Our Vision

To foster a supportive environment where students can freely articulate their feelings, receive support, and build a nurturing community.

Founder

Reena Wyz V. Betonio, a dedicated Grade 9 scholar at PSHS-SRC . At just 14 years old, Reena exemplifies a passion for academic excellence and an insatiable curiosity for learning. Her commitment to her studies and scholarly pursuits distinguishes her as a motivated and driven individual within the school community.

Values

Confidentiality, Supportiveness, and Inclusivity. These principles form the foundation of our community, fostering an environment where students feel secure, supported, and included in their academic journey.
